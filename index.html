FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase the bot?

To purchase the bot please contact us below. Payments are accepted in bitcoin and rarely in PayPal. Please add the creator as a friend on discord and have them contact you. We will get back to you as soon as possible.

How long does the bot take to install?

The bot is installed within two hours of payment and once the installation begins it will take roughly ten minutes.

How is the bot installed?

The bot is installed via team viewer by one of our professionals. To download team viewer please click here. The free version will get the job done so no need to pay any extra!

Wait, I thought the bot was on a website, why is an installation required?

You're correct it is website based, however, we also like to install a local version of the bot. This is just to guarantee that our users can use the bot at all times.

Can I share my link with my friends and tell them the answers?

Sorry, but no. Your link can only be viewed by one person at a time anyway. Please note sharing of bot answers will result in an instant ban from our bot. So just keep the answers to yourself.

Can the bot play international games and if so which ones?

Yes, the bot can play international games which are in foreign languages. It will translate both the question and the answers for you. At the moment the bot can translate Indonesian, Japanese, Mandarin, and French. Please note this feature is only available in our medium and pro packages.

Do I need to connect my phone to my computer?

No, you do not.

Which operating systems does this bot work on?

Any operating systems that run a web browser, we recommend chrome. If you are running the bot manually it works on Windows, Mac ,and Linux.
